# Privacy Policy

## X Undo Browser Extension

Last updated: January 16, 2026

### Data Collection

X Undo does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Timeline history (stored in IndexedDB)
- User settings (stored in browser's local storage)

This data never leaves your browser.

### Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

### Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:
- **storage**: To save your settings locally
- **Access to x.com and twitter.com**: To monitor and save timeline history

### Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
